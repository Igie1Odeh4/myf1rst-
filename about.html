About Aboveonly Microfinance Bank Limited

Aboveonly Microfinance Bank Limited is committed to delivering secure, innovative, and customer-focused financial solutions. We empower individuals, small businesses, and communities with accessible banking services, ensuring financial inclusion and growth. Licensed by the Central Bank of Nigeria (CBN), we prioritize trust, transparency, and excellence in every transaction, helping our customers achieve their financial goals.
