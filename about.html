About Aboveonly Microfinance Bank Limited

Aboveonly Microfinance Bank Limited is committed to delivering secure, innovative, and customer-focused financial solutions. We empower individuals, small businesses, and communities with accessible banking services, ensuring financial inclusion and sustainable growth.

Licensed by the Central Bank of Nigeria (CBN), we uphold trust, transparency, and excellence in every transaction while helping our customers achieve their financial goals.
